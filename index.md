# Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Radio FM application ("we", "our", or "the app") handles user information.

## Information Collection and Use

Radio FM does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information.

The app only streams publicly available radio stations through an internet connection. We do not require users to create accounts or provide personal data.

## Log Data

In case of app errors, basic technical information such as device model, Android version, and error logs may be generated automatically by the operating system. This information is used solely for app stability and improvement.

## Cookies

Radio FM does not use cookies.

## Third-Party Services

The app may connect to third-party radio streaming servers to play audio content. These services operate independently and have their own privacy policies.

Radio FM does not control or store data handled by third-party radio providers.
